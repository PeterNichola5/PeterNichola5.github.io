About Me

Hi! My name is Peter Nicholas and I graduated from CHCA highschool in the spring of 2021. I began learning how to code in my softmore year of highschool after joining the FIRST robotics team. There I learned the basics of java while on the controls team to help manipulate the robot.

As I grew to enjoy programming, I begin to seek out aditional learning material. Junior year I decided to take AP Computer Science, which improved my understanding of algorithms and data manipulation. By senior year, I began teaching myself C#, and begin messing around with my friend on Unreal Engine 4 in our school's computer lab. While our gaming project never got very far, playing around with Unreal taught me alot about problem solving, and inspired me to keep learning.

During my first semester of studying Astrophysics at the University of Cincinnati, I got into an acedemic slump and decided to take the spring semester off to recooperate. During this time, I spent the majority of my days either working a part time job or learning more about programming. With the help of some youtube tutorials, i began making a 2D Java game from scratch. I also enrolled in multiple classes through Codecademy, learning more about Java and C#.

After another semester at UC, I decided that I wanted to take a different path. I began the Codecademy Full-Stack Developer Career Path and hope to continue learning. I have grown a lot throughout my journey on learning to code, but I feel that there is still much growing to do.
